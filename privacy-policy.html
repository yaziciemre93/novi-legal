Privacy Policy for Novi

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Novi ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Novi is developed and operated by Emre Yazıcı, an individual developer based in Turkey.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos and Images

  • Collection: When you use Novi, you can upload or capture photos of rooms for AI-powered interior design generation.
  • Processing: Your photos are sent to Google's Gemini AI service for processing and design generation.
  • Storage: We do NOT store your photos on our servers. Images are processed temporarily and returned to your device only.
  • Retention: Photos are automatically deleted from processing servers immediately after generation is complete.

1.2 Analytics Data

  • We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app.
  • Data collected includes:
    • Screens visited
    • Features used
    • App crashes and performance metrics
    • Device type and operating system version
    • General location (country/city level, not precise location)
  • This data is anonymous and cannot be used to personally identify you.

1.3 Advertising Data

  • Novi uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.
  • AdMob may collect:
    • Your device's advertising ID
    • IP address
    • Device information
    • Ad interaction data
  • This data is used to show personalized ads based on your interests.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • AI Design Generation: To process your photos and generate interior design visualizations using Google Gemini AI.
  • App Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve app features and performance.
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads that support the free version of the app.
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

Novi uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: AI-powered image generation for interior design.
  • Data Shared: Your uploaded room photos.
  • Privacy Policy: Google Gemini API Terms

3.2 Google Firebase Analytics

  • Purpose: App analytics and performance monitoring.
  • Data Shared: Anonymous usage data, device information.
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

3.3 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Advertising to support the free app.
  • Data Shared: Advertising ID, device information, ad interactions.
  • Privacy Policy: AdMob Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Google (Gemini AI, Firebase, AdMob) as described above.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Protect Rights: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.

5. Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Photos are processed ephemerally and not stored on our servers.
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Ad Personalization

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Novi (toggle off)
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

6.2 Analytics Opt-Out

  • You can disable analytics tracking in your device settings.

6.3 Photo Deletion

  • Photos are automatically deleted immediately after processing.
  • Saved designs on your device can be deleted manually from the app.

6.4 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data.
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: yaziciemre93@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Novi is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States (where Google's servers are located).

These countries may have different data protection laws. By using Novi, you consent to such transfers.
